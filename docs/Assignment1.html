Take from 2

Zambia ranked 118th out of 137 countries on the 2018 Global Competitiveness Index, which looks at factors that affect economic growth and poor social indicators.

According to the office in Zambia of The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)3, the top 5 causes of death in Zambia (2018) are: 4

  1. HIV/AIDS
  2. Neonatal disorders
  3. Lower respiratory infections
  4. Tuberculosis
  5. Diarrheal diseases

Although the health problem generated by HIV does not distinguish race, gender or social status, developing countries, particularly those located in Africa, have this disease as one of the main health problems, Zambia is not the exception. Thus, the intervention proposal is on HIV / AIDS prevention.
